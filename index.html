YAMAHA DRUMS SERIAL NUMBERS


On every Yamaha Drum logo badge there are 2 sets of number/letter sequences. The model number of the drum is on the LEFT (IE: TT-912Y). The serial number will always be to the RIGHT of the model number.


NOTE: This will date drums from 1980 on only.


1980-1989:
Drums are coded with two-letter prefix on the serial numbers. The first letter in the serial number indicates the year of manufacture; the second letter is the month.


1990-Present:
Drums are coded with three-letter prefix on the serial numbers. The first two letters indicate year, the third letter indicates the month.
